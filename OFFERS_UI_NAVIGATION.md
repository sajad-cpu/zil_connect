## ğŸ“± Complete User Journey

### For Regular Users (Claiming Offers)

1. **Browse Offers**
   - Go to: `Offers` (main navigation)
   - See featured carousel + all offers grid

2. **Claim an Offer**
   - Click "Claim Offer" button
   - Modal opens with loading state
   - Unique coupon code generated (e.g., ZIL8X2K9)
   - Copy code button
   - Click "View My Offers" to see all your coupons

3. **View Your Claimed Coupons**
   - Go to: `Profile Menu â†’ My Claimed Offers`
   - Or: Click "View My Offers" in claim success modal
   - See all your coupons organized by status:
     - Active (ready to use)
     - Redeemed (already used)
     - Expired (past expiration date)

---

### For Business Owners (Creating Offers)

1. **Create New Offer**
   - Go to: `Profile Menu â†’ Create Offer`
   - Fill in:
     - Title & description
     - Discount percentage (1-100%)
     - Expiration date (optional)
     - Terms & conditions
     - Featured toggle
   - Submit

2. **Manage Your Offers**
   - Go to: `Profile Menu â†’ My Offers`
   - See analytics dashboard:
     - Total offers
     - Active offers
     - Expired offers
     - Total claims
   - View list of all your offers with:
     - Claim counts
     - Expiration status
     - Edit/Delete buttons

3. **Track Performance**
   - Each offer shows:
     - Number of claims
     - Created date
     - Expiration date
     - Featured status
